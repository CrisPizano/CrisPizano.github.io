FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions



Do I need an appt to come workout ? 

No appointments are necessary. Non-members can purchase a day pass prior to their vist on the home page of our website or in person upon check in. 
Members can stop by at anytime during our hours of operation from 5am-11pm.

Can I bring a guest? 

Guests are more than welcome, any day and any time. Guests are required to bring a local valid ID upon their visit to sign off on our liability waiver. 
Passes can be pre-purchased or pre-arranged.

How do I cancel ? 

Although we are terribly sad to see you go, we make canceling your membership as easy as possible. 
If you can make it in the club, we'd love to see you one more time for a final goodbye M-F from 8am-8pm or Saturdays from 8am-2pm. 
If you can't make it in, log-on to your member portal online to process your cancelation, visit: www.mypxmclubonline.com.
Your agreement should be out of term and in good standing in order to cancel, please provide a 14 day notice.
